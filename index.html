Our world is a gigantic data factory.

I'm Bartosz, and for the past 10 years, I've been trying to understand how this factory works.

Initially, my goal was to make computers work the way I wanted them to, so I became a software engineer. Soon, I realized these machines are not smart enough, so I became an AI engineer to teach them how to be more useful.

Working with AI allowed me to see that what truly matters is the information flowing through the world, or to be more precise, the data. From that moment, I got locked in the data engineering world.
